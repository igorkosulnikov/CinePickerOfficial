CinePicker

Made by Maxim Krylov, 2019

Description

CinePicker is a very simple app that helps you to find and pick movies.

The first idea is that having an account somewhere isn't so necessary to find and save cool films. You don't need to make one more pair of login and password somewhere else to have your personal movie list directly on your device. Just open the app and start picking.

Another idea is focusing on only what you need. CinePicker is not a jungle of unlimited screens and sections, tons of useless facts about famous Hollywood stars and so on. CinePicker is about movies. The app has minimalistic design, cool features and beautiful UI to make your search more plesant and easier than ever.

Features

  • Easy and quick search
  • Getting similar movies and movies in the series
  • Getting movies of a certain actor or some crew member
  • Saving movies in a few ways
  • Discovering movies by different attributes
  • Light and dark appearance
